AWARDS

Recognition Through Time

1991

Hong Kong Film Awards

Best Film & Best Director

Days of Being Wild (阿飛正傳)

Wong's breakthrough film swept the Hong Kong Film Awards, winning major categories including Best Film and Best Director, establishing his signature fragmented narrative style.

1997

Cannes Film Festival

Best Director

Happy Together (春光乍洩)

Wong's raw exploration of love and displacement in Buenos Aires earned him the prestigious Best Director award at Cannes, cementing his status as a master auteur.

2000

Cannes Film Festival

Technical Grand Prize

In the Mood for Love (花樣年華)

For Christopher Doyle and Mark Lee Ping-bing's breathtaking cinematography. The film's visual poetry redefined what cinema could express about memory and desire.

2001

César Awards

Best Foreign Film

In the Mood for Love (花樣年華)

France's highest film honor recognized Wong's masterpiece as Best Foreign Film.

2004

European Film Awards

Best Non-European Film

2046

Recognized for Wong's ambitious sci-fi tinged meditation on memory, love, and time.

2008
