About This Site

This is an unofficial fan-made tribute to Wong Kar-wai and his extraordinary body of work. All film rights belong to their respective copyright holders. This site is created purely out of admiration for one of cinema's greatest poets.

For official information about Wong Kar-wai's films, please visit the websites of Jet Tone Films and distributors such as The Criterion Collection.
